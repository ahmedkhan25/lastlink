In memory
A life remembered

Daniel Rourke

1962  –  2026
Father, carpenter, and the loudest laugh in any room. Dublin, Ireland.

Be kind. It costs nothing, and it outlives you.

Daniel built things with his hands and made everyone feel like the most interesting person in the room. He loved terrible puns, strong coffee, and his family — roughly in reverse order, though he'd never admit it.

He left messages for the people he loved, in his own voice, to be opened when the time came. This page is the public face of his goodbye; the private letters and videos reached each person directly.

Service & wishes
Saturday 14 June, 11:00
St. Mary's, Haddington Road, Dublin. No flowers, by his request — a donation to the hospice that cared for him means more.
